## About OTI Lumionics

OTI Lumionics focuses on the development of materials for the electronics sector, specifically for consumer electronics and automotive applications. The company provides materials for OLED displays, transparent displays, and integrated sensors and cameras, aimed at improving performance and efficiency. OTI Lumionics serves the consumer electronics and automotive industries. It was founded in 2011 and is based in Mississauga, Ontario.

OTI Lumionics, a leader in advanced quantum simulations and solutions for next-generation materials discovery, announces the publication of its scientific paper, “Optimization of the Qubit Coupled Cluster Ansatz on classical computers,” in the Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation (JCTC). The results mark a major advancement in the quantum computing and OLED industry, demonstrating that large-scale quantum simulations of materials can be simulated today on classical hardware with high-accuracy results. Historically, current quantum computers have struggled to achieve this capability.
