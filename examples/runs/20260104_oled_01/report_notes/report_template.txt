Guidance:
Template description: Nature Reviews-style technical overview.

Template tone: Formal, comprehensive, evidence-weighted.

Template audience: Researchers and R&D leaders.

Section guidance:
- Abstract: short summary of scope, key findings, and gaps.
- Introduction: define scope, terms, and historical context.
- Current Landscape: categorize approaches and evidence strength.
- Mechanisms & Methods: detail workflows, algorithms, or experiments.
- Comparative Evidence: contrast methods with pros/cons and data quality.
- Key Points: 4-6 bullet highlights (consensus + caveats).
- Challenges & Limitations: reproducibility, data limits, scaling issues.
- Outlook: near- and mid-term trajectories with decision questions.

Template writing guidance:
Use precise citations and clarify uncertainty.
Separate consensus findings from speculative signals.
