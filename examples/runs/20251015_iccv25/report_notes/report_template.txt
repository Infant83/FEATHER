Guidance:
Template description: Quanta Magazine-style narrative explainer.

Template tone: Narrative, conceptual, evidence-anchored.

Template audience: Broad technical readership.

Section guidance:
- Lede: 2-3 paragraphs; narrative hook; no bullets.
- Central Question: 2-4 sentences framing the mystery.
- The Story So Far: chronological narrative; key papers/actors.
- How It Works: conceptual mechanism; one equation only if clarifying.
- Why It Matters: scientific and human implications; cite sources.
- Open Questions: 3-5 questions; be explicit about uncertainty.
- Risks & Gaps: highlight missing evidence, constraints, unknowns, and validation needs. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
- Critics: short headline + brief paragraphs + bullets; include contrarian views. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.

Template writing guidance:
Use metaphors sparingly; avoid hype.
Keep warm, magazine tone without losing rigor.
