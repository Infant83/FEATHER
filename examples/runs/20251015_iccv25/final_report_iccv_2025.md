# ICCV 2025 Final Report

## Executive Summary
The ICCV 2025 conference serves as a pivotal platform for addressing the evolving challenges in computer vision, showcasing groundbreaking methodologies, particularly in Disentangled Representation Learning (DRL) that advance the field from theoretical frameworks to practical applications.

### Claim
The ICCV 2025 conference serves as a pivotal platform for addressing the evolving challenges in computer vision, showcasing groundbreaking methodologies, particularly in Disentangled Representation Learning (DRL) that advance the field from theoretical frameworks to practical applications.

### Evidence
The conference featured diverse papers focusing on transforming DRL from synthetic datasets to real-world applications, exemplified by the 1st International Workshop on Disentangled Representation Learning for Controllable Generation (DRL4Real). It presented innovative approaches in several key areas:

1. **Controllable Generation and Editing**: Papers demonstrated fine-grained control of generation tasks utilizing pre-trained models and semantic constraints, resulting in significant improvements in image editing accuracy.
2. **Integration of Diffusion Models**: Researchers explored new inductive biases and integrated diffusion models to enhance the robustness of DRL methodologies. Notably, the application of language semantics as a regularizer has shown promising results.
3. **3D and Sequential Disentanglement**: Techniques were developed to disentangle spatial and temporal factors in complex data, addressing challenges in areas such as autonomous driving and action recognition.
4. **Specialized Domains**: The workshop illustrated applications of DRL in specialized domains like EEG analysis for Brain-Computer Interfaces, emphasizing the versatility and possibilities of DRL in various fields.

These findings highlight the conference's commitment to advancing practical applications of DRL through rigorous discussions and explorations of state-of-the-art methodologies.

### Implication
The advancements presented at ICCV 2025 signify a critical shift towards integrating robust AI systems into real-world scenarios. The emerging trends in DRL not only enhance the controllability and interpretability of AI-generated content but also pave the way for future innovations in autonomous systems, healthcare, and beyond. This foundation builds a stronger framework for future research, aiming for practical solutions that address the complexities of real-world data.

In conclusion, ICCV 2025 has established itself as a cornerstone for researchers and practitioners dedicated to pushing the boundaries of computer vision, fostering collaborations that will drive the field forward.

## Key Findings
1. **Trajectory Prediction Enhancements**: 
   - 발표: Wei Xu 외, "Trajectory prediction via proposal guided transformer with out way attention"
   - 새로운 주의 메커니즘인 "Out Way Attention"을 도입하여 자율 주행 차량의 주변 행동 예측 정확성을 향상시킴.
 
2. **OpenCV의 응용**: 
   - 발표: Ekta Sinha 외, "OpenCV for Computer Vision Applications"
   - OpenCV를 활용하여 감시, 얼굴 인증, 가장자 감지 등 다양한 실시간 응용 프로그램을 탐색함.
 
3. **인공지능 및 임상 의사결정 지원**: 
   - 발표: Dyke Ferber 외, "Development and validation of an autonomous artificial intelligence agent for clinical decision-making in oncology"
   - GPT-4와 정밀한 종양학 도구를 이용한 자율 임상 AI 에이전트를 개발하여 높은 정확도로 임상 결론 도출.
 
4. **Vision-Language Modeling의 발전**: 
   - 발표: Xingxing Weng 외, "Vision-Language Modeling Meets Remote Sensing"
   - VLM의 강력한 성능과 구체적인 원격 감지 작업에서의 성과를 보임.
 
5. **Generative AI의 교육적 응용**: 
   - 발표: Yufeng Qian, "Pedagogical Applications of Generative AI in Higher Education"
   - Generative AI의 교육 시스템 내 변화를 평가한 체계적 문헌 검토를 진행.
 
6. **다중 모달 검색 시스템**: 
   - 발표: Bingbing Zhang 외, "Research on Multi-Modal Retrieval System of E-Commerce Platform"
   - 이미지와 텍스트를 이용한 보다 정확하고 효율적인 검색 시스템 개발.
 
7. **단기 비디오 참여 예측**: 
   - 발표: Dasong Li 외, "VQualA 2025 Challenge on Engagement Prediction for Short Videos"
   - 사용자 생성 콘텐츠의 인기도를 모델링하는 데이터셋 개발.
